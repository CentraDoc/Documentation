CDocRIP - simplified RIP interface

CDocRIP uses the PSI PostScript and PDF interpreters on top of a custom AntiGrain backend to produce rendered pages or bands.

Banding

The spec->info.Height will contain the number of rows in the band. Essentially the Width/Height of the output canvas should represent one strip or band.

PostScript files will rewind and reinterpret for every extra band for every page. DSC comments are not used to skip to specific pages due to the likelihood of failure. To avoid the overhead, distill the file to PDF first.

The CDocRip_DrawFirstBand and CDocRIP_DrawNextBand functions are for convenience and will automatically adjust the clip, place, and plotsize variables to accomodate the banding.

CDocRIP_DrawRect is similar to the banding calls but provides more control.

Note that the DrawNextBand and DrawRect calls will automatically seek to the next page if you try to draw a band off the bottom of the page, which returns an error. If you don't do this, you must manually skip to the next page with CDocRIP_SkipNextPage, or use CDocRIP_SetNextPage to specify your desired next page.
